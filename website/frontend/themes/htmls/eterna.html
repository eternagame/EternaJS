Welcome to Eterna!

Hello NOVA players and welcome to Eterna! The next steps on your RNA puzzle-solving journey are:



1. Register for Eterna. The 5,000 points you scored in the NOVA RNA Lab will be transferred to your Eterna Profile.

2. Put your RNA folding skills into practice by solving more challenging puzzles on Eterna.

3. Once you have reached a score of 10,000 points, enter the Eterna Lab and design RNAs that will be voted on by the worldwide Eterna community.

4. If you design a winning RNA, it will be made and tested by scientists to see if it folds in the way you predicted. The very best molecules will become the basis of a molecular toolkit to explore possible RNA-based therapies.

To learn more, check out the About Eterna  page.

Code of Conduct

Respect the Eterna Community

Eterna players come from different countries, age groups, institutions, schools, companies, teaching, online gaming, and scientific communities. As a part of the Eterna community, you are invited to discuss research topics, game experiences, competitions status, lab results, puzzle strategies, ideas, suggestions, and just to socialize! We trust you to be responsible in the community. While playing Eterna, please follow the community guidelines highlighted here. While using the Eterna site and chat:

  • Please do not display any violent, nude, racial, unsuitable pictures or posts, derogatory language, or links to adult content.
  • Please do not abuse, harass, or threaten any person.
  • Please do not send spams (unwanted messages) or virus links
  • Please do not behave like a troll

While off topic conversation is frequent in chat, it is generally not tolerated when it disrupts legitimate Eterna conversation.



These rules also apply to Username, About Me, Group Description, Player and Group profile pictures. Anyone who fails to follow the guidelines may be suspended or banned from Eterna. Remember that Eterna is your community! Each and every player helps Eterna get better. To report abuses of the Code of Conduct, please send a private message to our development team, who will keep your identity confidential.



Thanks for reading and understanding the guidelines!

The Eterna Team.
